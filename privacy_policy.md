## Overview

Ring Reminder is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate any servers, and we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data ourselves.

---

## Data collected and how it is used

### Data processed on-device only

The following information is accessed locally on your device to provide core app functionality. None of it leaves your device or is shared with us.

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Ringer mode, DND status, volume level | Detecting when your phone is silenced so alerts can be shown |
| Incoming call state | Triggering a visual pulse on the overlay when a call arrives while silenced |
| Notification categories | Detecting call-type notifications for the pulse feature (no notification content is read or stored) |
| Foreground app (Usage Stats) | Per-app suppression feature — suppressing alerts when a selected app is in use |
| App settings and preferences | Stored in Android SharedPreferences on your device only |
| Trial start date | Stored locally to determine your 7-day free trial status |

### Data we never collect

- Your name, email address, or any account information
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos, files, or media
- Microphone or camera data
- Any usage analytics or behavioural data

## In-app purchases

Ring Reminder offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade via **Google Play Billing**. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not receive, process, or store any payment card details or billing information. Google's privacy policy applies to that transaction: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Permissions explained

| Permission | Why it is needed |
|---|---|
| Draw over other apps | Display the alert overlay on your screen |
| Do Not Disturb access | Detect DND state and optionally unmute |
| Notification access | Detect call-type notifications for the pulse feature |
| App usage access | Per-app suppression (optional Pro feature) |
| Receive boot completed | Restart the monitoring service after a reboot |
| Battery optimisation exemption | Keep the background service running reliably |

## Third-party services

Ring Reminder does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs. The only third-party component is Google Play Billing, covered above.
